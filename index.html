How it works

1
Copy any TikTok link

Open TikTok, tap Share on any video, then tap Copy link. Works on any TikTok URL — tiktok.com, vm.tiktok.com, or m.tiktok.com.

2
Paste it into TikVoid

Tap the Paste button or long-press the input to paste your link. Then hit Fetch Video — we handle the rest in seconds.

Preview & save the video

Your video loads in the player — watch it, then tap Save to Device. The clean MP4 downloads straight to your phone or computer.
